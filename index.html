ABOUT US

Miada Local is an independent audiovisual production company based in Girona. It is dedicated to creating sustainable, community-focused projects with a strong social commitment. Its mission is to support local artists and generate content that positively impacts society, focusing on themes such as equality, sustainability, and human rights. The organization champions local talent, working to highlight authentic and meaningful stories. It employs responsible production practices and collaborates with local collectives and organizations that align with its values. One of its key objectives is to create audiovisual pieces directed at town halls and other public institutions, enabling them to use these materials in social campaigns and advertisements. Additionally, Mirada Local develops creative ideas for projects; these ideas are initially sold as concepts and, if acquired, are produced with full dedication and quality.
